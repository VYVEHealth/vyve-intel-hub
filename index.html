The VYVE Advantage

What nobody else in the UK market is doing — and why it matters to buyers

🎗️

1-in-5 Charity Pledge

For every 5 paid employees, VYVE provides free wellbeing support to 1 community member. Powered by the Everyman Charity — no other UK platform does this at scale.

⚡ Zero direct competitors in this space · Powerful CSR & PR differentiator
🔮

Proactive, Not Reactive

VYVE builds a 12-month wellbeing calendar — structured habits, events, and interventions before crisis hits. EAP players are purely crisis-response tools.

⚡ Shifts framing from cost centre → culture investment
🧩

Physical + Mental + Community in One Platform

All three pillars at SME price point. Bupa and AXA offer breadth but cost £15–60pp/pm. Unmind is mental-only. VYVE delivers the full stack at £5–14pp/pm.

⚡ Breadth at SME price = unique market position
🎙️

Expert Podcast + Speaker Network

The Everyman podcast and integrated speaker bureau give VYVE a content moat that no EAP or app-only platform can replicate.

⚡ No one else has a podcast-native model
🤝

Ambassador / Champion Programme

VYVE trains internal wellbeing champions inside client organisations — driving grassroots uptake without VYVE staff cost.

⚡ Drives retention and renewal from inside client orgs
🏙️

Community Events (In-Person)

Quarterly community events, Forest Hall FC partnership, charity fundraising — a human connection layer pure-app platforms cannot offer at VYVE's price point.

⚡ Nobody at VYVE's price combines digital + in-person community
